# Coastal prediction grid

A set of 948 prediction locations along the U.S. East and Gulf Coast at
approximately 15 km spacing, used for kriging predictions.

## Usage

``` r
prediction_grid
```

## Format

A data frame with 948 rows and 2 columns:

- lon:

  Longitude in decimal degrees (range: -97.7 to -67.0).

- lat:

  Latitude in decimal degrees (range: 25.0 to 45.1).

## Source

Sampled from Natural Earth 1:50m coastline (see White et al.).
